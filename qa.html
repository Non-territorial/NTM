NONTERRITORIAL MUSEUM

Q&A

What is NTM?

The Nonterritorial Museum (NTM) on Telegram is a platform that reimagines how art is curated, exhibited, and experienced, integrating exhibitions, marketplaces, user participation, and NFTs for unlimited art experiences.

Why?

NTM moves beyond the exhausted marketplace concept, mathematical imagery, and trading-only approaches, offering a fresh and dynamic art experience and distribution space.

What are the key functionalities?

NTM allows users to exhibit, host, experience, collect, expand their community, and enrich their existing profile.

Who can participate?

Any exhibitor with compatible content and any host with a reasonable audience can join the NTM space.

What is the role of the host?

Hosts become curators, dealers, galleries, and meaningful players in the art circulation and distribution space, bridging the gap between art and audience.
